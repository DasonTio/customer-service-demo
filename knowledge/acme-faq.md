# Acme Support — Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping

We ship to the United States, Canada, and the European Union. Standard
shipping takes 3–5 business days and costs $4.99. Orders over $50 ship free.
Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available for $14.99.

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking link by email. If your
package has not arrived within 10 business days, contact support and we will
open an investigation with the carrier.

## Returns and refunds

You can return any unused item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days
after we receive the return. Sale items marked "final sale" cannot be
returned.

To start a return, go to Orders → Select order → Return items, or ask a
support agent to generate a prepaid return label for you.

## Warranty

All Acme hardware products include a 2-year limited warranty covering
manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover accidental damage, water
damage, or normal wear and tear. Extended 4-year coverage can be purchased
within 60 days of the original order.

## Account and billing

You can update your payment method under Settings → Billing. We accept Visa,
Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Invoices are emailed at the start
of each billing cycle and are also downloadable from the Billing page.

If you see an unrecognized charge, contact support within 60 days so we can
investigate and, when appropriate, reverse it.

## Contacting a human

Human support agents are available Monday through Friday, 9am–6pm US Eastern
time. Ask the chat agent to "connect me with a human" and they will create a
ticket; average response time is under 4 business hours.
